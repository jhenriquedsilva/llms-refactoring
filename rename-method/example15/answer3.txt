#### Candidate Methods for Renaming

1. `pleaseMakeStackTracesClean()`
    - **Problem:** Looks informal and non-standard for a test setup method. Should be more direct and descriptive.
    - **Suggestion:** `setUpCleanStackTraces()`
2. `howMuchDidYouInherit()`
    - **Problem:** While understandable, it's a bit awkward in naming.
    - **Suggestion:** `getInheritedAmount()`
3. `getNameButDelegateToMethodThatThrows()`
